Happy Birthday Aaron ❤️

Two years have passed, yet somehow, my heart still finds its way back to you. Funny how time changes almost everything, but somehow, not the way I feel about you. It didn’t start as something grand. It was just simple conversations, random updates, shared laughter, and little moments that slowly became the best parts of my day. Without realizing it, you became my comfort person — the one I looked for in every notification, every quiet night, every thought I couldn’t explain. I still remember the small things. The pictures we took. The late-night talks. The chocolates, flowers, rides, random meetups, and moments that probably seemed ordinary to everyone else — but meant everything to me. There were times I tried convincing myself that it was just admiration, just a phase, just something temporary. But the truth is, the more time passed, the deeper I fell. Quietly. Softly. In the kind of way that doesn’t need loud words to become real. You became part of my everyday life without even trying. And maybe that’s why it hurt so much realizing how deeply I cared. Because suddenly, my days felt incomplete without you in them. And for those what we had and what will have — even if it was simple, even if it was unclear sometimes — felt genuine to me. You made my world softer. Happier. Warmer. You became someone I could love in silence and still feel grateful for. I love you sincerely. Even If life ever gives me another chance — in another time, another universe, another version of us — I hope I get to love you again. This time, without fear. Without confusion. Without almosts. Thank you so much for accepting me for who i am, for taking good care of me, for making me feel heard, seen and loved. Thank you so much for everything. No matter where life takes us, you will always be one of the best things that ever happened to me. Happy Birthday, baby. Here’s to more memories, more adventures, more laughter, and more years of loving you.
